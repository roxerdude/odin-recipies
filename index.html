Oatmeal A hearty bowl of oatmeal that will give you the necessary carbs to start your day! You can add any type of fruit to enhance the flavor.
  • Rolled Oats
  • Water
  • Heat Source
  1. Pour the oats into a bowl.
  2. Add a copious amount of water to the oats, it should look like a watery soup.
  3. Cover the bowl with a paper towel, and place it in the microwave for two minutes.
  4. The oats should now be ready for consumption.
